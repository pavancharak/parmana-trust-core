\# Policy Registry



\## Overview



The Policy Registry manages policy definitions used during authorization and trust evaluation.



Policies define the conditions under which authority may be exercised.



\## Responsibilities



\* Register policies

\* Retrieve policy versions

\* Preserve policy history

\* Support policy evaluation



\## Inputs



\* Policy Definitions

\* Policy Metadata



\## Outputs



\* Policy Records

\* Policy Versions



\## Trust Lifecycle Position



Task → Policy → Authority Decision
