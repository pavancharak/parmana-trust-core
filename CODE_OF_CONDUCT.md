\# Code of Conduct



\## Our Pledge



We are committed to making participation in the Parmana Trust Core community a respectful and constructive experience for everyone.



We pledge to foster an environment that encourages:



\* Professional discussion

\* Constructive feedback

\* Technical rigor

\* Respectful collaboration

\* Inclusive participation



\---



\## Expected Behavior



Community members are encouraged to:



\* Be respectful

\* Assume positive intent

\* Focus on technical merit

\* Provide constructive criticism

\* Help others learn



\---



\## Unacceptable Behavior



Examples include:



\* Harassment

\* Personal attacks

\* Discrimination

\* Trolling

\* Deliberate disruption

\* Publication of private information



\---



\## Project Principles



Parmana discussions should remain focused on:



\* Trust architecture

\* Verification

\* Authorization

\* Intent preservation

\* Execution trust

\* Independent verification



\---



\## Enforcement



Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, pull requests, or contributors whose behavior violates this Code of Conduct.



\---



\## Guiding Principle



Strong technical disagreement is acceptable.



Personal hostility is not.
