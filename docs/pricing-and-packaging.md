\# Parmana Pricing \& Packaging



\## Packaging Philosophy



Parmana is not sold as a cryptography product.



Parmana is sold as an Authority Verification Platform.



Customers do not buy attestations, receipts, or trust roots.



Customers buy:



\* Authority Verification

\* Policy Enforcement

\* Auditability

\* AI Risk Reduction

\* Compliance Readiness



The packaging should follow the maturity journey:



```text

Trust Core

&#x20;   ↓

Policy Registry

&#x20;   ↓

Authority Platform

```



\---

\## Purpose

Research.

Open source experimentation.

\* Receipts

Free
