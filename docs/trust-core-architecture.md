\# Parmana Trust Core



\## Core Principle



Humans define authority.



Organizations decide what to trust.



Parmana evaluates trusted signals against policy before execution and makes authority verifiable.



\---



\## Flow



Trusted Signals

&#x20;   ↓

Policy Evaluation

&#x20;   ↓

Decision

&#x20;   ↓

Attestation

&#x20;   ↓

Verification

&#x20;   ↓

Receipt

&#x20;   ↓

Transparency Log

&#x20;   ↓

Trust Root



\---



\## Responsibilities



Policy

\- Defines authority



Decision

\- Evaluates trusted signals



Attestation

\- Cryptographically binds decision



Receipt

\- Cryptographically binds verification



Transparency Log

\- Creates append-only history



Trust Root

\- Creates verifiable system state



Verification

\- Allows independent validation
